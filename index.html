Rage Against the (Vending) Machine

Welcome to RATVM, a student-run RCB project with one driving mission: to provide the snacks that YOU want, cheaply and conveniently.

To request a food or beverage, click here and let us know what you're craving! We will do our best to provide what the student body desires, because we know that a hungry coder is a distracted coder, and want to contribute to an atmosphere of academic success!
